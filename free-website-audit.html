Frequently Asked Questions

Is the website audit really free?

Yes, completely free with no obligation to hire me afterward. I review your site and send you a summary of what's working, what's broken, and what to fix first. If you want help implementing the fixes, that's a separate conversation you can decide on later.

What does the free website audit check?

The audit covers technical SEO (crawlability, indexing status, canonical tags, sitemap), Core Web Vitals and mobile page speed, on-page SEO (titles, meta descriptions, heading structure), schema markup presence, and whether Google Business Profile is set up correctly for local businesses. You get a prioritized list of what to fix first.

How long does a free website audit take?

Most audits are completed within 24-48 hours of submitting your website URL. For larger or more complex sites, it may take up to 3 business days. You'll receive the findings by email or WhatsApp, whichever you prefer.

Do I need to provide website login access for the audit?

No. The initial free audit only requires your public website URL. Everything needed for the audit (technical SEO, speed, indexing, on-page checks) is visible from the outside using tools like Google Search Console, PageSpeed Insights, and manual review. Login access is only needed later if you hire me to implement fixes.
