Kathmandu SEO: What Actually Works

What Kathmandu SEO Requires

  • Local keyword research in both English and Nepali
  • Google Business Profile optimization for local pack visibility
  • Competitor analysis of Kathmandu-based rivals
  • Technical SEO fixed before content or link building starts
  • Citations in Nepal business directories for local authority

Common Kathmandu SEO Mistakes

  • Targeting only English keywords, missing Nepali-language search demand
  • No Google Business Profile at all, a huge local pack miss
  • Building links before fixing technical SEO issues
  • Templates that load slowly on mobile, where 90%+ of Nepal traffic is
  • No schema markup, missing rich result and AI Overview eligibility

What makes SEO in Kathmandu different from other Nepal cities?

Kathmandu has the highest local business density and search competition in Nepal, and search intent splits between English queries and Nepali-language queries. Kathmandu businesses also compete heavily in the Google Maps local pack. An effective strategy needs keyword research for both language patterns, a fully optimized Google Business Profile, and technical SEO fixed before content or link building begins.

No. Web development and SEO implementation happen entirely in code and in your Google Search Console, Analytics, and Business Profile accounts, none of which require a physical office visit.
