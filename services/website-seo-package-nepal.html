FAQ

Why get a website and SEO from the same person instead of separately?
Building the site and SEO together means technical decisions (speed, schema, URL structure, internal linking) are made during development, not retrofitted afterward. This is typically faster and cheaper than hiring a developer, then a separate SEO consultant to fix what wasn't accounted for.
How much does a website and SEO package cost in Nepal?
The Website + SEO Launch Package starts around NPR 30,000. The Full Local Growth Package, which adds GBP setup and management, starts around NPR 45,000. Exact pricing depends on page count and content needs. Every project starts with a free website audit, no commitment required.
How long does it take to build a website with SEO included?
Typically 2-4 weeks from content collection to launch, similar to a website built without SEO consideration, since the SEO work happens alongside development rather than as a separate later phase.
Do I need a Google Business Profile if I already have a website?
Yes, for local businesses. A website drives organic search traffic, but GBP gets you into the local pack and Google Maps results, a separate part of Google's search results many Nepal businesses miss. That's why the Full Local Growth Package bundles both.
