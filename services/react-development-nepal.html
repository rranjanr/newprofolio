Next.js for SEO: The Advantage

For Nepal businesses that need Google to rank their pages, Next.js is the better choice. Server-side rendering means Google receives fully-rendered HTML not a blank page waiting for JavaScript. I've seen LCP improve from 4.2s to 1.8s just by migrating from CRA React to Next.js SSG.
