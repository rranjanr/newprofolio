FAQ

What does Google Business Profile management include?
Verifying and fully completing your profile, uploading photos regularly, posting weekly updates, responding to every review within 48 hours, monitoring your local pack ranking, fixing NAP consistency across directories, and a monthly report showing ranking movement and activity.
How much does GBP management cost in Nepal?
One-time setup and optimization typically costs NPR 8,000 to 20,000. Ongoing monthly management starts at NPR 10,000 per month, often bundled with broader local SEO work for better results.
Can you manage a GBP listing in Kathmandu if you're based in Biratnagar?

What's the difference between GBP setup and GBP management?
